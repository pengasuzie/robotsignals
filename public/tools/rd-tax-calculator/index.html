Australian R&D Tax Calculator for Bootstrapped Founders

If you're a bootstrapped founder paying yourself from your own savings or mortgage, the R&D tax refund isn't free money — it's a partial rebate on the tax you pay on your own salary. This calculator shows you the real net benefit, including cost of funding salaries and including the value of superannuation.

Watch Out

  • Arm's lengthYour salary must be what an independent person would pay for the same role. The ATO can disallow R&D deductions if the salary is inflated. $60–150k is generally safe for technical founders. salary: $60–150k is safe for most technical founders. Above that, document market comparables.
  • $20,000 minimum: Your total R&D expenditure must reach $20,000 to qualify for the refundable offset. Below that, you can't claim.
  • Backdated salary risk: If you backdate salary and super payments, overdue super triggers the Super Guarantee Charge — the amount owed plus interest and admin fees. Pay on time.
  • Refund goes to the company: The R&D refund lands in your company bank account, not your personal account. You need a mechanism (dividend, loan repayment, salary) to extract it.
